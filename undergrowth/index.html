Privacy Policy — The Undergrowth

Last updated: February 27, 2026

This privacy policy describes how The Undergrowth ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile game.

1. We Do Not Collect or Hold Your Data

All your data stays on your device. Your game progress (resources, upgrades, prestige level) is stored locally. We have no servers that receive or store any user data. We do not collect, hold, or track any information about you.

2. Advertising Data (Google Only)

Our app uses Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to show ads. We do not receive or have access to this data. Google AdMob may collect identifiers and usage data on their own (device identifiers, IP address, ad interaction data) to deliver ads and measure performance. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

App Tracking Transparency. On iOS, we may request permission to access the Advertising Identifier (IDFA) for personalized ads. You can decline; ads will still be shown but may be less relevant.

3. How We Use Information

We use no information at all. Local data is used only by the app on your device to run the game and save your progress. Advertising data is used solely by Google AdMob. We do not sell, share, or transmit any personal data.

4. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Google Mobile Ads — for in-app advertising. Any data collection is done by Google; we do not receive or access ad data. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit — for in-app purchases. Transactions are processed by Apple; we do not receive payment or billing details.

5. Data Retention

Local game data remains on your device until you reset it via the in-app "Reset" option or uninstall the app. We retain nothing. Ad-related data is collected and retained by Google according to their policies.

6. Your Choices

You can:

  • Opt out of personalized ads via the iOS App Tracking Transparency prompt.
  • Reset all local save data from Settings → Reset.

7. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

For questions or support, use our Support & Feedback form.
